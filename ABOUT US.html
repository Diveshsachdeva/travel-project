Accurate Holiday is one of the preeminent travel company based in Delhi. A small idea of its founder has made it one of the best travel service providers in India and overseas. The travel agency has earned many rewards from the travelers in the form of their belief in us. The company is the one stop solution for all your travel needs.

We provide facilities such as hotel booking, flight booking, Volvo booking, and car packages and in complete holiday packages at your door step within an hour.

Accurate Holiday basically covers main sectors of Domestic and International. Within India we deal in Himachal Pradesh in North India, Kerala in South India and Goa in West and Kashmir in North Part of India. At International level the company provides the packages of Bangkok, pattaya, Malaysia and Singapore.

All the efforts and initiative of the company rotate around the travelers and the soul goal of the company is to intensify your travel experience with the top quality services. We work as a travel guide for you available any time with the complete travel information that is reachable from any part of the world.

Our belief is to render the excellent services to the traveler to attain full satisfaction level of the traveler. Accurate Holiday is there to make your travel economical and unforgettable. To accurately plan your holiday our dedicated team work together to plan your trip. We are specializing in providing highly customization tour plans according to the requirement of the traveler. We work according to you because to attain you is our target.
